Pakistan's premier orchid flowers

Orchids are a striking and alluring flower type that comes in a wide variety of blooms. The exotic nature of the flowers is guaranteed to impress anyone. Orchids are considered as a symbol of class and grace to add a soft outlook to any floral or decor blend. There are between 20 and 30 thousand species of orchids in the world and around 70 thousand hybrid species of this magnificent plant. Some of the types include phalaenopsis, dendrobium, cattleya, vanda, cymbidium, oncidium as well as lady slipper orchid. Orchids are perennial flowers and available in all shades and color combinations, never missing the onlookers’ attention and not to forget the soothing smell that works like positive therapy.

Orchids are flowers of merriment and positivity, they bloom year-round, therefore if you wish to have a blooming orchid at home at all times, it is recommended to buy multiple blooming plants during different times of the year. Orchids are available in different rich and vibrant colors such as green, white, purple, red, pink, yellow, and orange. They also come in a variety of different tinted colors, blue and hot pink being the most popular one. Each color of an orchid symbolizes a different emotion and green stands for good fortune, white stands for purity, blue stands for contemplation, purple signifies respect, red symbolizes passion, pink signifies femininity, yellow symbolizes friendship while orange represents boldness.
