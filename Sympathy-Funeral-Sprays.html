Comforting Funeral Wreaths and Casket Sprays

Funeral sprays are reserved for close family members to show their sympathy.

Funerals are calm and remorseful occasions, bidding a final farewell to the departed, wishing the soul a good afterlife and tranquil solace. Not only candles and framed photos are ritually presented at funerals but flowers also hold a high significance in spreading solace and tranquillity at the time of final service. There are different funeral sprays that you can present on the grieving day in the form of wreaths, standing arrangements, bouquets and even caskets. Flowers lighten the tone of the occasion, making it positive and spreading the sweet scent in the air. Flowers make a sad day peaceful.

Want to customize your bundle of funeral flowers and adornments? You can do so when you get the casket or the easel set up with Flower Co. and you can add more flowers or the flowers of your choice. You can even choose from the different natural and artificial adornments you want to grace your floral presentation with, such as ribbons, exotic foliage, poms, hypericum berries, eucalyptus seeds, etc. You can choose the style by making it a wreath, standing arrangement, casket, or even a trail, it is all on you and whether you want to offer it to the departed or the departed’s family and relatives.

Want to present blossoming sprays for funeral and different arrangements to the deceased’s family making it a memorable and fragrant ceremony. Take your pick from our floral blends or get your bundle of flowers customized with different adornments and blooms.
